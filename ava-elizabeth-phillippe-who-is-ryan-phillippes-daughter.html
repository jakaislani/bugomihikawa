Who is Ava Elizabeth Phillippe?

Ava Elizabeth Phillippe is an American model, but she is perhaps best known to the world as the daughter of famous actors Ryan Phillippe and his ex-wife, Reese Witherspoon. Ava has just started her career, but has already worked with a number of popular brands, including Tiffany and Co, Chanel, and others.

Ava Elizabeth Phillippe Wiki- Age, Childhood, and Education

Ava Elizabeth Phillippe was born on the 9th September 1999, in Los Angeles, California USA; she has a younger brother, Deacon Reese Phillippe, born in 2003.

Her parents divorced in 2007, and both of them have children from other relationships, and as a result, Ava has a half-sister, Kaliani Merizalde, from her father’s romance with Alexis Knapp, and a half-brother, Tennessee James Toth, from her mother’s marriage to Jim Toth. She has been raised by both parents as they have shared custody of her and her brother, and she has remained close to both Reese and Ryan. For her education, Ava attends the University of Berkeley, California, but she hasn’t shared what she’s studying.

Career

Ava, although a daughter of parents who are both actors, she hasn’t pursued an acting career; instead, she has been focused on the fashion and modeling side of show business.

She started her Instagram page and began uploading pictures of herself, which attracted not only fans but also talent agents and scouts. She was soon signed up to a modeling agency, and right after she had her first job as a model, recruited by Tiffany and Co to promote its line of jewelry. Ava steadily became more popular, and more interested in fashion, and made her catwalk debut in 2018 appearing in the look book for Rodarte’s Fall 2018 collection. After this, she became the face of the Draper James brand, a clothing line founded by her mother Reese Witherspoon, and modeled her mother’s creations for the Spring 2018 collection. Since then, Ava has become an Instagram influencer, with over 880,000 followers on her official page.

She has also displayed her other skills, including drawing, sculpting, sketching, painting and other craftwork.

Ava Elizabeth Phillippe’s Father, Ryan Phillippe

Born Matthew Ryan Phillippe on the 10th September 1974 in New Castle, Delaware USA; he is the son of Richard Phillippe, a chemist, and his wife Susan, who operated a daycare center in the family home.

Of French ancestry, he spent his childhood with three sisters. He went to New Castle Christian Academy, located in Wilmington, Delaware.

Career and Net Worth

Ryan’s career started in the early ‘90s with the role of Billy Douglas in the highly popular soap opera “One Life to Live”. He enjoyed success throughout the decade, with roles such as Gil Martin in the adventure-drama film “White Squall” (1996), then the lead role in the drama film “Little Boy Blue” (1997), co-starring Nastassja Kinski and John Savage. Before the ‘90s ended, Ryan had the two most famous roles in his entire career, the first in the horror film “I Know What You Did Last Summer”, and then in the romantic drama film “Cruel Intentions”, in which he co-starred next to his future wife.

He continued successfully into the new millennium, starring in a number of films, of which the most popular are the Academy Award-winning comedy mystery drama “Gosford Park” (2001), then the Academy Award-nominated war drama “Flags of Our Fathers” (2006), and the historical drama “The Bang Bang Club” in 2010. Most recently, Ryan starred in the TV action-drama series “Shooter” (2016-2018).

Ryan has appeared in more than 60 films and TV series, and his net worth is estimated at $35 million, as of early 2020.

Ava Elizabeth’s Mother, Reese Witherspoon Short Bio

Born Laura Jeanne Reese Witherspoon on the 22nd March 1976, in New Orleans, Louisiana USA, she is the daughter of John Draper Witherspoon and his wife Mary Elizabeth ‘Betty’ (Reese).

She went to Harpeth Hall School in Nashville, and was a cheerleader. She enrolled at Stanford University, an English literature major, however, she left before graduating, deciding to pursue acting as a profession.

Reese Witherspoon Career, Wealth, and Second Marriage

Reese started her acting career in the early ‘90s, having previous experience as an advertising model when she was seven years old. Her official debut was in the film “Man in the Moon” (1991), followed by the co-lead role in the television movie “Wildflower”. She starred in several more films in the ‘90s, including the comedy crime-drama “Freeway” (1996), then the thriller drama “Fear” (1996), and “Election” (1999), in addition to “Cruel Intentions”.

She enjoyed her best years in the early 2000s, with the part of Elle Woods in the films “Legally Blonde”, and its sequel “Legally Blonde 2” in 2001 and 2003, and in 2005 with the role of June Carter in the biopic “Walk the Line”, for which she won the Academy Award. Her acting career declined in the next few years, but she has bounced back with the lead role in the film “Wild” (2014), and then with another main role, this time in the TV series “Big Little Lies”. Reese is also a producer and has her own production company Hello Sunshine.

Her wealth is estimated at $200 million, as of early 2020.

After divorcing Ryan, she started dating Jim Toth, whom she married in March 2011. Since then, the couple has welcomed a son, Tennessee James, born in September 2012.
