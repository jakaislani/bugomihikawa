Who is Ana Gasteyer?

Ana Gasteyer is an American actress. She is popular for her performance on ‘Saturday Night Live’ from 1996 to 2002. Also, she is well known for her roles in ‘Suburgatory’, ‘Lady Dynamite’, and ‘People of Earth’.

Who are the parents of Ana Gasteyer?

She was born on May 4, 1967, in Washington, D.C., USA. Her birth name is Ana Kristina Gasteyer and she is currently 54 years old. Ana’s mother’s name is Mariana Roumell-Gasteyer and her father’s name is Phil Gasteyer. Her mother is an artist and her father is a lobbyist who became the mayor of Corrales, New York.

Ana Gasteyer holds American citizenship and her ethnicity is a mix of English, German, Greek, and Romanian.

Ana Gasteyer: Education, School/College University

Ana Gasteyer graduated from Sidwell Friends High School and later graduated from Northwestern University. She earned B.Sc. in speech, theater, and performance studies from the Northwestern University. Ana was a member of The Groundlings comedy during her university time.

Ana Gasteyer: Professional Life and Career

Ana Gasteyer debuted her acting career from the starring role of a woman in an American sitcom ‘Seinfeld’ in 1995. Then, she appeared in the series ‘Saturday Night Live’ from 1996 to 2002. She also made her appearances in the TV shows like ‘Hope and Gloria’, ‘Party of Five’, and ‘NYPD Blue’.

Ana Gasteyer has debuted her feature film from the role of a mother in the film ‘A Small Domain’. She appeared in the films like ‘Meet the Deedles’, ‘Mad About You’, ‘Robot & Frank’, ‘Fun Size’, ‘Peeples’ and recently, she portrayed the role of Catherine in the film ‘Wine Country’ in 2019.

In addition, Ana Gasteyer is also portraying the voice role of Reirei in ‘The Lion Guard’ since 2016, portrayed the main role of herself in ‘the $100,000 Pyramid’ in 2016, and she gave the voice of Krolia in ‘Voltron: Legendary Defender’ in 2018.

Ana Gasteyer: Awards, Nominations

Ana Gasteyer was nominated for BTVA Television Voice Acting Award in the category Best Female Vocal Performance in a Television Series in a Guest Role for ‘The Lion Guard’ in 2017 and nominated for ShowWest Award in the category Ensemble Award for ‘Geography Club’ in 2014.
