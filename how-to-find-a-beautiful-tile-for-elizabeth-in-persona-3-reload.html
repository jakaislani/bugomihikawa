News: If you really like playing Persona 3 Reload, you probably understand how crucial it is to discover tricky items and finish quests. There’s this one quest where you need to find a nice tile at the Mahjong Parlor Red Hawk in Port Island Station. This guide will help you locate the tile, increase your Courage stat, and get a Scrub Brush reward from Elizabeth..

Heading 1: Journey to the Mahjong Parlor Red Hawk

To start your journey, head to Port Island Station. Once you arrive, make your way to the Mahjong Parlor Red Hawk. This is where you will find the beautiful tile you’re looking for. However, it’s not as simple as just walking in and grabbing it. You’ll need to complete a few tasks first.

Heading 2: Challenge the Youths

When you enter the parlor, you’ll notice two youths standing near the entrance. Speak to them, and they will challenge you to a game of rock-paper-scissors. This is where your Courage stat comes into play. To increase your chances of winning, make sure your Courage stat is high enough before attempting this challenge.

Heading 3: Obtain the Beautiful Tile

After beating the two youths in rock-paper-scissors and winning, they’ll hand over the lovely tile to you. Now that you’ve got the tile, it’s time to take it back to Elizabeth. You can find her in the Velvet Room, so just head over there to finish the quest.

Heading 4: Reward from Elizabeth

When you present the tile to Elizabeth, she will reward you with a Scrub Brush. This item can be useful for cleaning and maintaining your weapons, so it’s definitely worth the effort to complete this quest.

Finding the beautiful tile at the Mahjong Parlor Red Hawk in Persona 3 Reload can be a rewarding experience. So, embark on this quest and make the most of your Persona 3 Reload gameplay. Happy hunting!

Frequently Asked Questions

A: Elevate your Courage stat by participating in various activities in the game, such as studying at the library or providing correct answers during class.

A: you have the flexibility to tackle this quest at any stage of the game. Yet, it’s advisable to have a higher Courage stat for increased success in the rock-paper-scissors challenge.
