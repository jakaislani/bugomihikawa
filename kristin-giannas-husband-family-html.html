Kristin Giannas Is a Family Person and One Proud Sister of a Cop

 Posted on September 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  499 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

American meteorologist Kristin Giannas timely shares her family details on her social media besides relaying news about weather and road conditions. She has been vocal about her family ties, especially with her brother Willian Giannas, a decorated cop.

However, amidst all the personal details, there isn't a single time the traffic anchor has referred to a husband, a boyfriend, or anyone remotely associated with her romantic life.

Giannas Grew up in a Blaring Household

Born to a Greek father and an Italian mother, Giannas grew up in a family with two distinct cultures. 

She shared her early life during an introductory episode of Good Day Orlando, describing her household as loud yet endearing. She stated that they all yelled, even when talking to one another normally. 

Giannas's parents have been married for 36 years.

On their 36th marriage anniversary, the meteorologist wished them via her Instagram handle on July 21, 2020. She showed her appreciation towards them and joked about the family yelling and their thick New York accents. 

For a family where yelling is a part of life, celebrations take on an entirely jestful meaning, replete with dancing and merrymaking.

Her family dances at the Greek festival every year, and whenever there's Christmas, they prepare special treats like Kourabiedes, the Greek almond biscuits.

Her Brother Is a Decorated Cop

Giannas has two brothers — Willian Giannas and Peter Giannas.

Giannas's brother William serves as an officer in the Clearwater Police Department in Florida. Before serving as a police officer, he was an army man.

The Fox 35's anchor has frequently posted about him, including on National Law Enforcement Appreciation Day and Veterans Day. Even her Facebook profile picture features her and her brother in uniform.

William was awarded the Medal of Meritorious Service with fellow officer Anastasia Leeman in July 2020 for helping a homeless family.

According to the Clearwater Police Department, the two officers came across a homeless family and paid for a hotel room to get them out of the cold.

William and Anastasia split the cost of the hotel room and bought the family two large hot pizzas. The two also provided a list of family-friendly shelters the family-of-three could visit the next day.

On William's birthday, on May 30, 2021, Gianna shared his picture in uniform and shared that she was proud to be his sister. She also praised him as one of the strongest people she knew. 

 Her other brother Peter is also making her proud by studying medicine at the University of Florida.

Is Kristin Giannas Married or Dating Anyone?

Unlike how open she is with sharing details about her family, her social media is void of her romantic life. She has not been romantically associated with anyone, or at least not publicly.

Her passion and dedication, so far, have solely been to her family and her career. A part of the Good Day Orlando crew, Giannas has been involved in broadcast journalism since 2007.

With a degree in telecommunications and news broadcasting from the University of Florida and a certificate in climatology and meteorology, her work in the realm of weather reporting has been commendable.
