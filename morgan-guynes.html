Demi Moore's Half Sibling Morgan Guynes 

Morgan Guynes, aka Morgan Shands Guynes, was born on 26 July 1967 in the United States of America. He was born to father Danny Guynes (1943 - 1980) and mother Virginia Beverly King (1943 - 1998). 

He grew up in his hometown with his parents and his half-sibling, Demitria Gene Demi Guynes. 

Guynes' step-sister Demi (1962) is an American actress and film producer best known for her work in movies like G.I. Jane, Charle's Angels: Full Throttle, Ghost, and Indecent Proposal.

Besides that, he is also the former half-brother of Ashton Kutcher, Freddy Moore, and Bruce Wills. 

Regarding his education, Morgan Guynes went to Fairfax High School, Los Angeles.  

Morgan Guynes: Former Marine Corps To Special Effects Technician

Morgan Guynes served as a Marine Corps CID agent. His service station was in Kuwait during Operation Desert Shield / Storm.  

After he retired from the Marine Corps, he started working as a special effects technician in 1992. 

He has worked with the Marvel Cinematic Universe, DC Comics, and other prestigious American media franchises. Some of his best works, which were box-office hits, are Oblivion, Marvel's Captain Marvel, Spider-Man: Homecoming, Avengers: Endgame, Avengers: Infinity War, Guardians of the Galaxy: Vol. 2, Green Lantern, G.I. Joe: Retaliation, and many more. 

As of 2020, Morgan Guynes has around 40 special effects credits. 

How Much Is The Net Worth Of Morgan Guynes?

After Two Marriages, Morgan Guynes Is Now Settled With Robin Stevens

Morgan Guynes is a married man not only once but thrice. 

Guynes is currently married to Robin Stevens. The lovebirds exchanged their marital vows on 12 March 2015 in an intimate wedding ceremony. 

As a matter of fact, Robin Stevens is Morgan's third wife. Previously he was married twice. 

Firstly, he was married to Lesley Guynes, with whom he has two children, identical twins Cooper Guynes and Oliver Guynes. 

Both of his sons are actors known for their recurring appearances in the CBS soap opera, The Young and the Restless. 

After his divorce from Lesley Guynes, he walked down the aisle with Julie Witherington. She is a set decorator and assistant property master from the film industry. 

Julie and Morgan met each other on the sets of their common movies. Soon they started dating and were married. But, their married life took a wrong turn, and they divorced. 

As of now, special effects technician Morgan Guynes is living a blissfully wedded life with Robin Stevens.
