Medikal

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  262 words  |  Martina Birk

Popularly known on stage as Medikal, Samuel Adu Frimpong, a Ghanaian hip hop musician cum rapper, was born on 4 April 1993.

Medikal attended his high school at Odorgonno Senior High and is the son of James Frimpong and Portia Lamptey.

Samuel adopted the name Medika because he was fascinated with rapping about doctors, surgeons, medical practitioners and hospitals in general.

He comes from Sowutuom, a town in the suburb or Accra in Ghana. Medikal and Sarkodie had the highest nominations for the 2017 edition of the Ghana Music Awards In 2018, he was on the Tim Westwood Show.

He has made a name for himself in the music industry through hard work and his numerous collaboration with successful artists.

Apart from is nominations in 2017, he won the best discovery video in the MTN 4Syte Video Award in 2016.

Also know, as MDK, AMG, Medikal, has had incredible music career, getting several Awards and nominations both locally and internationally.

Below are the awards and nominations he has received and won in the year 2017.

Afro Pop Song Of The Year – Nominated
Hip Hop Song Of The Year- Nominated
Hip-life/hip Pop Artist Of The Year- Nominated
Best New Act -Nominated
Best Rapper of the year -Nominated
Best Collaboration of the year- Nominated
Overall Artist of the year- Nominated

Additionally, Medikal won himself Hip Hop Artist of the year and that of Best Rapper of the year in 2019 at the Ghana Vodafone Music Awards (VGMA).

The Rapper married his girlfriend Precious Fella Makafui on 7th March 2020 in Accra- Ghana.
