Why You’ll Love This Cocktail

Experience the magic of the holiday season with this simple cranberry cocktail that’s as easy to drink as it is to make! Here’s why you’re going to love it as much as I do:

  • Festive Flavors: Cranberry and orange are my favorite holiday flavors and this cocktail perfectly balances the two while adding the smooth, nutty almond flavor of amaretto.
  • Party Perfect: This recipe is the perfect cocktail recipe for parties. With it’s 1:1:2 ratio it’s easy to multiply up for punch bowls, pitchers – whatever you need. Great for a fancy holiday party or a casual gathering!
  • Just 3 Ingredients: You simply can’t go wrong with a 3 ingredient cocktail! It’s simple enough to whip up a single glass after dinner or make up a big punch bowl for a party.

How To Make This Easy Christmas Cocktail

Rim Glass

This is totally optional. Rub an orange wedge along the edge of your glass or dip into a bit of orange juice. Dip the rim of the glass into a shallow bowl or plate with sugar. Set aside.

Prepare Cocktail

  • Fill glass halfway with ice.
  • Combine cranberry juice, amaretto (and vodka if using), and orange juice in a cocktail shaker. Shake well.
  • Pour over ice into glass.
  • Garnish with cranberries and orange slices if desired. Enjoy!

    • Storage Information

    To make ahead of time, combined the cranberry juice, amaretto and orange juice and refrigerate for up to 24 hours.

    When ready to serve, add vodka if using and serve over ice or transfer to a pitcher or punch bowl when ready to serve.

    Variations To Try

    • Add a cinnamon stick or a dash of ginger for a warm, spicy note.
    • Garnish with a sprig of rosemary or mint for an aromatic touch.
    • Try a cranberry blend juice for added flavor and depth.
    • Top with soda, ginger ale or sparkling wine for a fizzy finish.

    Trish’s Tips

    • Chill the ingredients so the ice doesn’t melt too quickly and dilute the cocktail.
    • Consider freezing cranberries to add to the punch bowl for a festive finish.
    • Use colored sugar or even cinnamon for the sugar rim.
    • Grab pulp free orange juice for a super smooth cocktail.

    Can I use cranberry juice cocktail ?

    Sure! It will make the cocktail a bit sweeter and less tart than if you use 100% Cranberry Juice.

    Can I make this cranberry cocktail in larger batches?

    Sure! Here is a breakdown for a 2 to 2.5 gallon punch bowl (keeping in mind you will have ice and garnishes):

    Without Vodka

    • 10 cups cranberry juice
    • 5 cups Amaretto
    • 5 cups orange juice

    With Vodka

    • 8 cups cranberry juice
    • 4 cups Amaretto
    • 4 cups Vodka
    • 4 cups orange juice

    Cranberry Christmas Cocktail

    This easy Christmas Cocktail is both festive and refreshing – the perfect way to celebrate this holiday season! This merry mix combines tart cranberry juice, nutty amaretto, and a bright splash of orange juice for a signature cocktail that is perfect for holiday gatherings and cozy nights in. Add a splash of vodka for even more Christmas spirit!
