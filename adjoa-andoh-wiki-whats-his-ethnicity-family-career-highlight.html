Adjoa Andoh HonFRSL is a British actress. She has been on stage in prominent roles with the Royal Shakespeare Company, National Theatre, Royal Court Theatre, and Almeida Theatre. She played Francine Jones in two seasons of Doctor Who, as well as 90 episodes of the renowned BBC medical drama Casualty and EastEnders.

Andoh made her Hollywood debut in Clint Eastwood’s drama picture Invictus in the autumn of 2009, as Brenda Mazibuko, Nelson Mandela’s head of staff, alongside Morgan Freeman as Mandela.
Since 2020, she has portrayed Lady Danbury in the Netflix medieval romance series Bridgerton. In July 2022, Andoh was named an honorary fellow of the Royal Society of Literature.
