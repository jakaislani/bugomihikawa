Will Ospreay Is Elated That TNA Wrestling Is Back

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  320 words  |  Martina Birk

IMPACT Wrestling is finally bringing TNA Wrestling back. IMPACT’s announcement has elevated the excitement of wrestlers and fans alike. People are expecting total non-stop action from the company. Will Ospreay is one such talent who is elated by the news.

It was announced at the IMPACT Wrestling Bound For Glory pay-per-view that IMPACT will be rebranded as TNA starting from the Hard to Kill pay-per-view.

At the Bound For Glory event, the Aerial Assassin was also featured on the show. He wrestled Speedball Mike Bailey. The two put on a stellar fight and in the end, Ospreay picked up the win.

The current IWGP United States Champion took to Twitter to share his support for the Nashville-based promotion. He mentioned that he had one of the best experiences as a fan watching TNA and now is very happy that it is back.

“I have had the best time at @IMPACTWRESTLING from fan experience to quality of wrestling. I honestly believe that the reason they have climbed back from such a deep trench… is because that place is one big team. The moment they said “we are TNA” I said “yes they are”,” Will Ospreay tweeted.

TNA Kids

Ospreay also shared his excitement for the people working in the company and is looking forward to the future.

“I’m so happy for all of that crew because they all work so hard and are grateful for the fan support. TNA was the reason I wanted to become a wrestler. With that team, they will inspire brand new TNA kids,” He tweeted.
